Strategic Foundation

Strategy Disclaimer

Booster Source is a long-term strategy to reposition Booster Juice as a transparent daily nourishment brand; to essentially explain that Booster Source is a strategy for essentially a new Booster Juice; Booster Source is NOT the new brand, but the essence of it.

Booster Source organizes wellness around visible nutritional value, clearer sourcing, and more responsible systems.

The Foundation

Booster Source is not just a brand pivot; it is an operational commitment to transparency. By making ingredients, sourcing, and material responsibility visible at every touchpoint, we transform from a generic wellness provider into a trusted daily partner.

Strategic Rationale

As guests prioritize real nutritional value over vague health claims, Booster Source provides a logical, grounded framework for decision-making. We eliminate the 'magic' and replace it with repeatable, functional benefit.

4. Operationalizing Strategy

Team-Based Guidance

The Booster Source strategy is executed across four key functional areas. Each team is responsible for specific outcomes that build toward our overall transparency goals.

Design & Creative

Maintain the "Grounded & Premium" aesthetic. Prioritize clarity over decoration. Use modular systems that allow ingredient stories to lead.

Marketing & Voice

Adopt the "Useful & Transparent" tone. Reduce hype-based claims and replace them with specific, source-based benefits.

Operations & Ops

Streamline material systems. Ensure disposal pathways are consistent across store footprints to back up responsibility claims.

Franchise & Training

Empower store teams with ingredient knowledge. Simplify recommendation logic to help guests find their "daily gap" solution.
